About Osagie Academy

Founded in 1995, Osagie Academy has grown from a small community school to one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Our mission is to provide a holistic education that develops intellectual, physical, emotional, and social competencies in our students, preparing them to be responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in academic excellence and character development.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Creativity, and Respect for all.

Admissions

Join our vibrant academic community by following our seamless admissions process designed for both primary and secondary students.

How to Apply

  • Obtain and fill the application form (apply online or at the school).
  • Submit the completed form with required documents.
  • Attend an interview or entrance exam (as scheduled).
  • Receive admission decision within 1 week.
Online Application Form Download Application Form

Requirements

  • Birth certificate or age declaration.
  • 2 recent passport photographs.
  • Previous school reports or transcripts.
  • Completed application form.
